### 🏫 Web Development & UI/UX Design: Little Bloomer Academy

A warm, gentle, and modern landing page designed and developed for **Little Bloomer Academy**. The project focuses on creating a sweet, parent-friendly user experience with soft pastel aesthetics, smooth transitions, and intuitive navigation.

*   **Project Type:** Full-Stack Web Development & UI/UX Design
*   **Tagline:** "We Prepare Your Child For Life"
*   **Target Audience:** Parents and Guardians looking for a nurturing early education environment.
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   Crafted a warm, kid-friendly visual identity utilizing soft earth tones, custom illustrations, and rounded, friendly UI elements.
    *   Fully responsive navigation featuring sections for *Home*, *Features*, *About*, *Programs*, and *Teachers*.
    *   Highly visible and user-friendly call-to-actions, including a prominent "Enroll Now" portal.
